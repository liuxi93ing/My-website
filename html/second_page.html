Here is about my daily life

My life may not be exciting or splendid, but it is enriched and peace. Research has taken most parts of my life. In my spare time, I used to play tons of video games just as other teens. My favorites are strategy games and I have been playing Hero3 up till now. I hit the gym regularly if time permits and I have finished half-marathon twice so far. Living in Calgary, skiing has gradually become my main outdoor activity during endless winter. Honestly, it could quite boring living abroad alone. Fortunately, a cutie has always been accompanying me who makes my life colorful. We visited many places, either for astonishing scenery or for taste of local cuisine. What's more, I like reading, especially fiction and history. But I feel ashamed of the number of books I read recently.
